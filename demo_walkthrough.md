# Demo Walkthrough

## Recommended 2 to 4 minute flow

The strongest flow now is macro to micro:

1. use `Portfolio Rankings` to find a compelling organization fast
2. sync that organization into the shared selector
3. move through the original 4 tabs to explain why that organization matters

## Recommended timed script

### 0:00 to 0:20

Open the app and leave the default organization in place.

What to say:

- "This is a judge-facing resilience dashboard for Fairlight Advisors."
- "It uses our retained upgraded logistic model and the `peer_relative_composite` label."
- "The new portfolio tab lets us move from macro scanning to org-level action in one flow."

### 0:20 to 0:55

Go first to `Portfolio Rankings`.

What to click:

- Set `Ranking mode` to `Fragile but investable` or `Highest shock sensitivity`
- Keep `Top N` at `25`
- If you want a fast macro filter, narrow by one peer group or risk bucket
- In the selected-row panel, choose `ALLIANCE FOR AGING INC (EIN 65-0101947)` or another top-ranked row
- Click `Use selected org in main dashboard`

What to say:

- "This tab is the macro control room."
- "Instead of clicking one nonprofit at a time, we can rank the full portfolio by baseline risk, reserve weakness, leverage pressure, shortlist class, or scenario sensitivity."
- "Once we find a strong candidate, we send it straight into the rest of the workflow."

### 0:55 to 1:35

Move to `Risk Score Lookup`.

What to click:

- Point at the baseline distress probability tile
- Point at the risk bucket
- Point at the driver chart and threshold chips

What to say:

- "Now we are at the org-level explanation layer."
- "The score is not a black box. We can see reserve weakness, leverage pressure, concentration stress, and retained threshold triggers."
- "That makes the risk legible in operating terms."

### 1:35 to 2:05

Move to `Peer Benchmarking`.

What to click:

- Point at reserve percentile
- Point at leverage percentile
- Point at the peer comparison table

What to say:

- "Against comparable peers, we can see exactly where this organization is weak or strong."
- "That helps Fairlight frame the first intervention conversation around liquidity, leverage, or concentration rather than generic advice."

### 2:05 to 2:45

Move to `Shock Simulator`.

What to click:

- Select `Donation shock (-30%)` or `Expense inflation (+10%)`
- Point at the before / after tiles
- Point at the scenario table and most-sensitive-scenario message

What to say:

- "This is a deterministic stress test, not a causal forecast."
- "It tells us who becomes urgent under pressure, not just who looks weak today."
- "That makes the portfolio story more decision-useful."

### 2:45 to 3:25

Move to `High-Impact Discovery`.

What to click:

- Show `Fragile but investable`
- Show `Resilient outperformers`
- Show `Shock-sensitive watchlist`

What to say:

- "This tab turns analytics into action buckets."
- "Fragile but investable cases are where support can matter."
- "Resilient outperformers are peer-learning examples."
- "The watchlist catches organizations that can jump under stress."

### 3:25 to 4:00

Optional close back on `Portfolio Rankings`.

What to click:

- Switch the ranking mode from `Fragile but investable` to `Resilient outperformers` or `Largest bucket jump under scenario stress`

What to say:

- "The portfolio tab makes experiment storytelling easy."
- "We can compare ranking lenses live without changing the underlying model."
- "That gives judges a fast macro view and Fairlight a practical shortlist workflow."

## Best demo settings for the new tab

If you only have one minute on the portfolio tab, use exactly this:

- `Ranking mode`: `Fragile but investable`
- `Scenario type`: `Worst case across scenarios`
- `Scenario ranking metric`: `Absolute risk delta`
- `Top N`: `25`
- `Sort direction`: `Descending`

If you want the fastest macro story:

- Start on the portfolio tab
- sync one organization into the shared selector
- explain it with Tabs 1 through 3
- finish on the shortlist tab
